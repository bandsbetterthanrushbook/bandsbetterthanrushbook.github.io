After years of painstaking investigation, countless hours of listening, and an unwavering dedication to the truth — Erik Burton has produced the definitive answer to one of rock music's most hotly debated questions.

About the Author
Erik Burton
✦

Erik Burton is a passionate music enthusiast and long-time Rush devotee. With a deep appreciation for the band's unique sound, cultural impact, and the sheer audacity of a Canadian power trio taking over the world, Erik set out to create something truly special.

After years of exhaustive research — involving careful examination of all available evidence — he arrived at the only conclusion a reasonable person could reach. Bands Better Than Rush is the result of that journey: his love letter to Geddy, Alex, and Neil, presented in the most honest format possible.

The book is available exclusively on Amazon.
